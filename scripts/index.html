The problemGetting paid shouldn't mean doing accounting

Freelancers, creators, and small teams do the same thing every time money comes in: set some aside for savings, pay a collaborator, track it all by hand. It's repetitive and easy to get wrong. ORYN does it automatically — you describe the rule once, and it runs itself from then on.

New to crypto wallets? Read this first.

A wallet is just an app (like MetaMask) that holds your money and lets you approve transactions. Nobody, including ORYN, can move funds from it without you personally approving each action.

cUSD is a digital dollar (1 cUSD ≈ $1) that runs on Celo, a fast, low-cost payment network. It's the currency ORYN actually moves.

Non-custodial means ORYN never holds or controls your money — it only has permission to execute a split you already approved, and that permission can never be used to send funds anywhere you didn't authorize.

For anyone getting paidHow it works

Five steps, and only two of them ever need your attention again after setup.

1

Get your vault

A vault is a smart contract that only you control — think of it as your personal, programmable bank account on Celo. One vault per person.

2

Describe your split in plain English

"Pay my designer 30%, rest stays as savings." ORYN turns that into a rule you review and sign yourself — nothing is guessed silently.

Open Set Rules in the app
3

Share your payment link

Send anyone who owes you money a link to this page. They pay in cUSD, from any wallet, in a few clicks.

Open Deposit in the app
4

ORYN executes the split automatically

Within seconds of a deposit, the agent detects it and runs your exact rule onchain — no manual step, no delay.

5

Withdraw your savings, anytime

Whatever isn't allocated to a rule sits safely in your vault as savings. Pull it out whenever you want.

Open Withdraw in the app

Paying someone via ORYN?You don't need a vault — just a wallet

If someone asked you to pay them through ORYN, here's everything you need. No sign-up, no account.

  • A wallet that can connect to a website (MetaMask or similar) — it'll prompt you to add Celo automatically if needed.
  • A little CELO for gas — a fraction of a cent covers it.
  • cUSD for the amount you're paying. Don't have any? Swap CELO for cUSD non-custodially at app.mento.org, or ask whoever you're paying for their vault's address.
